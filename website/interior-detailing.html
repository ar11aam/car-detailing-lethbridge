Interior Detailing

Premium Interior Detailing

Experience top-quality car detailing at your home or workplace in Lethbridge! Our expert team ensures your vehicle's interior looks spotless and feels fresh.

Our interior detailing service is designed to thoroughly clean, sanitize, and protect all interior surfaces of your vehicle, restoring that new car feeling.

What's Included

Deep Vacuuming

Thorough vacuuming of all carpets, seats, and hard-to-reach areas to remove dirt and debris.

Steam Cleaning

High-temperature steam cleaning to sanitize surfaces and remove stubborn stains from upholstery.

Air Vent Cleaning

Detailed cleaning of air vents to remove dust and improve air quality inside your vehicle.

Leather Treatment

Specialized cleaning and conditioning of leather surfaces to prevent cracking and maintain softness.

Interior Glass Cleaning

Streak-free cleaning of all interior glass surfaces, including windows and mirrors.

Surface Protection

Application of UV protectant to dashboard and trim to prevent fading and cracking.
